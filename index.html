Language Models, Information, Networks, Knowledge, and Society

Bridging language model research and social science to understand how AI is reshaping the networks of information and knowledge that society depends on.

About

The Workshop on Language Models, Information, Networks, Knowledge, and Society (LINKS) brings together researchers from NLP, machine learning, and the social sciences to examine how large language models are reshaping the information networks and bodies of knowledge that underpin society. Topics include political bias and civic discourse, misinformation and fact-checking, AI agents and content moderation, epistemic autonomy, and governance.
